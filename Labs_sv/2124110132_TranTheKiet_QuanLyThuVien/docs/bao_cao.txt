Bao Cao: Giai Thich Thuat Toan Quan Ly Thu Vien

1. Thuat toan them sach:
   - Su dung danh sach lien ket de luu tru cac sach.
   - Ham addBook se tao mot node moi va them vao cuoi danh sach.

2. Thuat toan tim kiem sach:
   - Duyet qua danh sach va so sanh tieu de hoac tac gia.

3. Thuat toan xoa sach:
   - Tim node can xoa va cap nhat con tro.
